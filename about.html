About Me

Hi! My name is Eric. I was born and raised in Chicago Heights, Illinois; a southern Chicago suburb.

As a first generation Hispanic student, paving my way through higher education has been a whirlwind of experiences and emotions. Beginning a journey where no relatives have passed through can be an intimidating process since guidance is limited. However, in 2017 I took a leap of faith to explore my potential and seek greater opportunities through the world of academia and technology.

Today, I am a Senior at Illinois Institute of Technology pursuing a B.S in Computer Science. Through internships, personal projects, and course projects; I have gained experience and proficiency in AWS, Terraform, Python, Linux, and Git. I also have familiarity with the Agile/Scrum methodology, CI/CD methodology, and the Infrastructure as Code (IaC) practice.

I am interested in Internship/Co-Op opportunities specializing with the Cloud ☁️: automation, analytics, engineering. I value companies with a diverse culture and who consistently provide a space for their employees to learn, be challenged, and grow professionally and individually.

Outside of these things, I am an avid non-fiction reader and podcast binger. I enjoy learning about various topics outside of tech through these resources. Most of these topics are things I am genuinely interested in such as human biology, holistic medicine, and nutrition science. A typical week for me consists of exercising, cooking, walking my dog (she's a Black Mouth Cur!), and hanging out with family and friends.
